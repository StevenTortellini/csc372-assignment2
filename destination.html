Paris, France

Overview

Paris, often called the “City of Light,” is one of the most visited cities in the world. Known for its history, art, and cuisine, it offers an unforgettable experience for travelers. From charming cafés to world-class museums, Paris is a city that blends tradition and modernity.

Must-See Spots (Hover for hours)

  1. The Eiffel Tower
  2. The Louvre Museum
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral
  4. Le Arc de Triomphe
  5. Montmartre and the Sacré-Cœur Basilica

Visitor Tips

  • Learn a few basic French phrases to connect with locals.
  • Purchase museum passes to skip long lines.
  • Wear comfortable shoes for exploring on foot.
  • No plan is sometimes a good plan just go with the flow.
